# CrestForge Unit Abilities (40 Units)

## Ability Design Notes
- Each unit has one active ability that triggers when mana is full
- Damage/healing scales with star level (1.5x at 2-star, 2x at 3-star)
- Two units have major crowd control: **Naga Wizard** (3-cost) and **Skeleton Mage** (5-cost)

## Suggested Combos

### "CC Lock" Composition
1. Get **Naga Wizard** (3-cost) for accessible stun
2. Build toward **Skeleton Mage** (5-cost) for the ultimate freeze
3. Add **Ice Golem** to slow enemies between CC abilities
4. Pair with high burst damage (Beholder, Demon King) to capitalize on CC windows

### "Healing Fortress"
1. **Cleric** heals entire team
2. **Flower Monster** keeps your carry alive
3. **Mushroom** and **Starfish** provide sustained healing
4. **Castle Monster** taunts and shields

### "Assassin Strike"
1. **Rat Assassin** dives backline
2. **Flying Demon** follows up on backline
3. **Werewolf** leaps for damage
4. **Worm Monster** burrows for disruption
5. All Momentum/FirstBlood synergy!

### "Burn Everything"
1. **Flame Knight** for massive fire area damage
2. **Salamander** for cone damage
3. **Demon King** for team-wide burn
4. **Red Slime** for explosion on death
5. Volatile trait makes deaths hurt enemies too!
