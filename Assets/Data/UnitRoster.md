## Suggested Team Compositions

### "Explosion Squad" (Volatile + Gigamega)
- Red Slime, Mushroom, Evil Plant, Beholder, Bone Dragon
- Skeleton Mage, Flower Monster, Specter for Gigamega
- Big ability damage, units explode on death

### "Tank Wall" (Bruiser + Mitigation)
- Blue Slime, Worm Monster, Golem, Ice Golem, Castle Monster
- Green Spider, Bishop Knight, Spiky Shell Turtle for Mitigation
- Extremely durable frontline

### "Kill Chain" (FirstBlood + Momentum + Overkill)
- Little Demon, Rat Assassin, Werewolf, Orc with Mace, Demon King
- Bat, Flying Demon for Momentum
- Snowball through combat with kills

### "Eternal Army" (Forged + Bruiser)
- Salamander, Flame Knight, Golem, Lizard Warrior, Black Knight, Castle Monster
- Scale permanently through the game
- Strongest late-game composition

### "Resource Engine" (Scavenger + unique traits)
- Red Slime, Crawler, Fishman, Naga Wizard, Evil Old Mage
- Add Chest Monster (2-cost) for Treasure, Blacksmith (2-cost) for Crestmaker
- Easy early access to economy-generating uniques!

### "Holy Light" (Rare Holy comp)
- Cleric (3-cost) + Blacksmith (2-cost) as core (only 2 Holy units!)
- Pair with Attuned units for elemental synergy
- Invigorating keeps team alive
- Blacksmith available early to start crafting crests

## Notes

- **Little Demon** (1-cost) and **Naga Wizard** (3-cost) have 3 traits including Attuned - great for early trait activation
- **Green Spider** has both Reflective AND Mitigation, making it an excellent early defensive unit
- **Spiky Shell Turtle** is the ultimate tank with the same combo at 5-cost
- **Cleric** (3-cost) and **Blacksmith** (2-cost) are the only Holy units - rare and valuable
- **Chest Monster** and **Blacksmith** are 2-cost uniques - available early to shape your strategy!
- **Flame Knight** (5-cost) explodes on death and spreads excess damage - devastating finisher
- **Fury** is the rarest shared trait (4 units) - harder to hit (4) breakpoint
- Units with **Scavenger** help generate economy through free units
- Units marked with * in trait distribution have 3 traits
